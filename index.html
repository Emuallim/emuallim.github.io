About Me

My name is Muhiddin and I love front-end development but I also get a kick out of anything design and web related. I enjoy figuring out how things work and making thoughtful, engaging, and functional web applications that have a clean and simple design.

I’ve built web applications using HTML5, CSS3, Bootstrap, Javascript, JQuery and React and have a foundation in web design and development. I've recently completed 300 hours of “Responsive Web Design Projects” certification at www.freecodecamp.org and I’m coding to earn their “Verified Front End Development Certication.”

Before moving into development, I worked as an English teacher almost for 9 years and helped more than 600 students get into top international universities in Uzbekistan and abroad. Since I like to challenge myself from time to time, I took the IELTS (International English Language Testing System) exam and got an overall band score of 8.0 out of 9.0 with a perfect score of 9.0 in speaking to join the top 2% of non-native English speakers of the world as a self-taught language learner.

I can’t wait to reach the same level of fluency in computer languages, especially in Javascript.
