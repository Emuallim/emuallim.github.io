PLAY SPANISH

You are going to learn Spanish in a completely different and fun way. All you got to do is just 4 things:

  1. Learn vocab. through actions
  2. Learn a story in 100% Spanish
  3. Test your Spanish through quiz
  4. Retell the story in your words

To retain the vocabulary words easily, you must do the actions (some words don't have actions) & retell the story in your own words!

Enjoy!

VOCABULARY

Repeat the action 3x!

(1) Es - is

STORY

Watch 3x!

Paco es una person de Espania. Paco es un jugador professional de tennis. Un dia, Paco va a la tienda porque quiere uno zapatos Nike. Paco compra lo zapatos Nike.

QUIZ

Paco es de Mexico si o no?

100%

RETELL

See? Language learning can be easy and fun if you use the right method!

Now, for this story to be engraved on your memory, you must retell the story in your own words!
